When a blood test for polio had to go to the CDC for testing and the person had to be paralyzed for more than 60 days, polio numbers dropped overnight.

DDT was outlawed at the same time polio numbers dropped. The countries that continue to have high polio numbers are those where DDT is still legal - like India.
